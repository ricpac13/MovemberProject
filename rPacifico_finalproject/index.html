WHAT WE DO

Cancer Awareness

Around the world, 43 men die from prostate cancer every hour. We’ve pushed prostate cancer research forward 50 years, but there’s much to be done. Testicular cancer is the most commonly diagnosed cancer in young men. A 95% chance of survival isn’t good enough, not for the 1 man in 20 who faces a lethal outcome, and not for us.

Mental Health Awareness & Suicide Prevention

Every minute a man dies from suicide. We need to talk and take action to reduce the rate of male suicide. There’s no single reason why men take their own lives, but we do know that by improving overall mental health we can reduce the risk of suicide. Help us turn the tide on mental health.

Get More Men Talking

Health isn't something that sits high on men's priority list but it should. More young men die from testicular cancer every day, Older men are at a higher risk of prostate cancer after age 64, More men need to open up about mental health and suicide as well as talk about these issues so less men suffer in silence.

Movember Foundation

With the support of our community, The movember foundation is funding innovative, world-class projects in prostate cancer, testicular cancer, mental health and suicide prevention.

In the video, I explain my history with the foundation, Talk a bit about my personal story, Explain why this foundation is so important and why more men need to get tested early and start talking about these issues.
